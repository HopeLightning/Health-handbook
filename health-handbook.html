❤️ 心肺功能：一切健康的基础 Cardiovascular: The Foundation of Health
  • ▸心血管就像身体的发动机，心肺功能差，患高血压、糖尿病等慢性病风险显著升高Your cardiovascular system is your body's engine — poor cardio significantly raises risk of hypertension, diabetes, and chronic disease.
  • ▸高强度训练需要有一定基础才能做。运动基础薄弱时贸然做高强度训练，反而可能增加风险。建议先把心肺功能练到及格线以上，再考虑进阶。High-intensity training requires a fitness foundation. Attempting it without one increases risk rather than reducing it. Build your cardio to at least the passing level first.
  • ▸关键指标是最大摄氧量（VO₂max），用心率手环可以测Key metric: VO₂max, measurable with a fitness band or smartwatch.

🏃 训练要点 Training Guidelines
  • ▸强度判断：Gauge intensity:稍微喘气，但还能说完整句子；上气不接下气 = 过量，立刻停下You can talk but slightly breathless = right intensity. Out of breath = too much, stop.
  • ▸推荐运动：Recommended:快走 / 跑步机坡走（速度4-6公里/小时）Brisk walking / incline treadmill walking (4-6 km/h)
  • ▸训练频率：Frequency:练一天休息一天，休息日可以慢走做积极恢复Train one day, rest one day; active recovery (slow walk) on rest days
⚠️

重要前提：必须睡好觉！睡眠不好的时候不要做强度训练，等状态恢复了再练。Sleep is foundational. Never do intense training when sleep-deprived — wait until you recover.

🤸 为什么柔韧度很重要 Why Flexibility Matters
  • ▸柔韧度下降会出现：梳头挠后背困难、手臂抬不起来（五十肩）Loss of flexibility causes difficulty reaching behind your back, frozen shoulder, and reduced range of motion.
  • ▸关节要在灵活和稳定之间找平衡，影响日常生活的质量Joints need a balance of mobility and stability — flexibility directly affects quality of daily life.
  • ▸柔韧度改善比肌肉训练更快，先练这个更容易获得成就感Flexibility improves faster than strength — start here for early wins and motivation.

📊 肌肉的三个维度 Three Dimensions of Muscle
  • ①肌肉耐力：Muscular endurance:肌肉能维持动作的时间（如平板支撑）How long muscles can sustain an effort (e.g. plank hold)
  • ②肌肉力量：Muscular strength:肌肉能发出的最大力量（如一次深蹲的最大重量）Maximum force muscles can produce (e.g. max squat weight)
  • ③肌肉量：Muscle mass:肌肉总重量（举铁主要练这个维度）Total muscle weight (primarily built through resistance training)
🦵 肌肉耐力：上坡走路 Endurance: Incline Walking

自测：Self-test:无负重深蹲，匀速蹲起30次，做不到说明需要练。Bodyweight squat: 30 reps at steady pace. If you can't, this training is for you.

  • ▸核心训练：Core exercise:上坡走路，速度4-6公里/小时，站在跑步机两个把手中间Incline walking, 4-6 km/h, standing between treadmill handles
  • ▸姿势要点：Form:肚脐一直指向前方，保持髋关节稳定Keep navel pointing forward, hips stable
  • ▸加大步幅：Bigger strides:让更多肌肉参与，训练效率更高Engages more muscles, higher efficiency
  • ▸脚尖向前：Toes forward:别内八外八，避免膝盖损伤Avoid toe-in/toe-out to prevent knee injury
  • ▸扁平足：Flat feet:可以买50-200元的鞋垫调整Orthotic insoles (~$10-40) can help
💪 肌肉力量：为什么要练 Strength Training: Why It Matters
  • ▸应对突发：Emergency response:地铁急刹能稳得住、摔倒时能自己撑起来Stay steady on a sudden subway stop; get up after a fall
  • ▸保护关节：Protect joints:跑步时膝盖要承受3倍体重，力量够才不会伤关节Running loads knees with 3× body weight; strength prevents injury
  • ▸抗衰老：Anti-aging:从30岁到70岁，臀大肌、大腿、腹肌、背肌的粗细和力量会掉一半，比化妆品有效得多From age 30 to 70, glutes, quads, abs, and back muscles halve in size and strength. More effective than cosmetics.
